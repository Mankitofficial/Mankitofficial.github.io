About Me

My name is Mankit, born and brought up in Karnal. I am currently a student of PM Shri Government Senior Secondary School, Karnal and a dedicated JEE aspirant, working consistently towards academic excellence and intellectual growth.

I am a proud member and alumnus of the Prerana Program, an initiative supported by the Ministry of Education, Government of India. This program has played a significant role in shaping my perspective on leadership, responsibility, and nation-building.

I am also an active member of the Sakura Science Club, Japan, which has helped me develop a global outlook on science, innovation, and collaborative learning. These experiences have strengthened my belief in disciplined thinking and continuous improvement.

I strongly believe in equality, dignity, and respect and openly identify as a feminist. Alongside academics, I engage as a motivational speaker, sharing thoughts on self-discipline, clarity of purpose, and responsible youth leadership.

My journey is guided by consistency, integrity, and the courage to work silently towards meaningful outcomes, while allowing results to speak louder than words.
