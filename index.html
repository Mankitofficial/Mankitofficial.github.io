About Me

My name is Mankit, born and raised in Karnal. I completed my schooling from PM Shri Government Senior Secondary School, Karnal.

I identify as a feminist, respect all religions equally, and believe that every success in my life belongs to my parents, teachers, the Prerana family, and some very special friends.

This portal is dedicated to self-growth and to all those people whose contribution I want to acknowledge throughout my life.
