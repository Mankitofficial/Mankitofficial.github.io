About Me

My name is Mankit, born and raised in Karnal. I completed my schooling from PM Shri Government Senior Secondary School, Karnal. I am currently a JEE aspirant who believes in consistency, discipline, and honest effort.

I am an alumnus of the Prerana Program, supported by the Ministry of Education, Government of India, and a member of the Sakura Science Club, Japan.

I identify as a feminist and hold deep respect for all religions and belief systems. I believe equality, dignity, and mutual respect are essential values.

All the success I have achieved so far is credited to my parents, school teachers, the Prerana family, and some very special friends — mentors, teachers, boys and girls — who guided and supported me.

This portal is dedicated to self-reflection and to those individuals whose contribution I sincerely value in my life.
